Welcome to Snake FunZone!

How to Play:

  • Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control the direction of the snake.
  • The objective is to eat the food that appears on the screen. Each time the snake eats the food, it grows longer.
  • Be careful not to collide with the walls or the snake's own body, or the game will end.
  • Try to achieve the highest score possible by eating as much food as you can!

Press any key to start the game. Good luck and have fun!
